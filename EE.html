About Emergency Emden
Located by the north sea in Germany, Emden is a port city that attracts both big companies and criminals alike.

=Keep the city safe from crime as police officer,

=Fight raging fires and rescue people as firefighter,

=Save people's lives as a paramedic,

=Bring people around the city in a large bus.

=Purchase and drive a wide array of different civilian cars, ATVs and motorcycles.

=Customize them and change their paint and rims; add ornaments, spoilers and underfloor lighting to make your vehicle visually appealing and unique.

=Turn against the law and serve yourself on the money of the local bank; rob gas stations and parked money trucks to make your living in an illegal way.

=But watch your back for other traitor criminals and responding police forces!

=Play in a private server and customize the map to create unique roleplaying, racing or PvP events for your friends and community.
